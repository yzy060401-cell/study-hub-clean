Week 1

Foundations of Business and Financial Decision-Making

企业基础与财务决策导论

Key Concepts

  • Business / firm ｜ 企业 / 公司
  • Financing, assets, operations, revenue ｜ 融资、资产、运营、收入
  • Financial accounting vs management accounting ｜ 财务会计与管理会计
  • Shareholders, board, managers ｜ 股东、董事会、管理层
  • Ownership and control ｜ 所有权与控制权
  • Limited liability ｜ 有限责任
  • Healthcare business examples ｜ 医疗行业案例

Core Logic

  • A business raises finance, buys assets, uses them to deliver goods or services, and earns revenue. ｜ 企业先融资，再购买资产，用资产提供产品或服务，并获取收入。
  • To be financially viable, revenue must be enough to cover costs. ｜ 企业要持续经营，收入必须足以覆盖成本。
  • Financial accounting is mainly for external reporting, while management accounting supports internal decisions. ｜ 财务会计主要服务外部报告，管理会计主要支持内部决策。
  • Managers use accounting and finance information to decide on investment, financing, pricing, and control. ｜ 管理者用会计与财务信息来做投资、融资、定价和控制决策。

Important Distinction

  • Owners are not the same as managers. ｜ 所有者不等于管理者。
  • Accounting is not only record-keeping; it is also decision support. ｜ 会计不只是记账，也是一种决策支持工具。
  • A profitable-looking business can still face funding or cash problems. ｜ 看起来赚钱的企业也可能面临融资或现金流问题。
  • Healthcare organisations still face business decisions, even when their mission is social or clinical. ｜ 即使目标偏社会或临床，医疗机构仍然要面对商业决策。

Exam Thinking

Week 1 is not just definitions. Strong answers explain how finance, assets, operations and decision-making connect in a real organisation.

第1周不只是背定义。高质量答案要说明融资、资产、运营与决策在真实组织中如何连起来。

Week 2

Financial Statements I: Balance Sheet & Income Statement

财务报表 I：资产负债表与利润表

Key Concepts

  • Balance Sheet / Statement of Financial Position ｜ 资产负债表
  • Income Statement / Profit and Loss ｜ 利润表
  • Assets, liabilities, equity ｜ 资产、负债、权益
  • Revenue, expenses, profit ｜ 收入、费用、利润
  • Gross profit, operating profit, net profit ｜ 毛利、营业利润、净利润
  • Depreciation ｜ 折旧

Core Logic

  • The balance sheet is a snapshot at one point in time. ｜ 资产负债表反映某一时点的财务状况。
  • The income statement measures performance over a period of time. ｜ 利润表衡量一段时间内的经营表现。
  • Assets = Liabilities + Equity is the core accounting relationship. ｜ 资产 = 负债 + 权益 是核心会计恒等式。
  • The two statements are linked: profit can increase retained earnings. ｜ 两张报表是联动的，利润会影响留存收益。

Important Distinction

  • Income statement shows flow; balance sheet shows position. ｜ 利润表看“流量”，资产负债表看“存量”。
  • Not every cash-related item belongs on the income statement. ｜ 不是所有和现金有关的项目都进利润表。
  • Depreciation is an expense, but not an immediate cash outflow. ｜ 折旧是费用，但不是当期真实现金流出。
  • Dividends are not the same as operating expenses. ｜ 股利和经营费用不是一回事。

Exam Thinking

In statement questions, do not just list items. First identify what belongs to performance, what belongs to financial position, and how they connect.

做报表题时不要机械罗列项目。先判断哪些属于经营表现，哪些属于财务状况，以及它们之间如何连接。

Week 3

Financial Statements II: Cash Flow & Ratio Analysis

财务报表 II：现金流与比率分析

Key Concepts

  • Statement of Cash Flows ｜ 现金流量表
  • Operating / investing / financing activities ｜ 经营 / 投资 / 融资活动
  • Cash vs profit ｜ 现金与利润
  • Liquidity ｜ 流动性
  • Profitability ratios ｜ 盈利能力比率
  • Efficiency ratios ｜ 效率比率
  • ROCE, margin, turnover ｜ 资本回报率、利润率、周转率

Core Logic

  • Cash flow statements show where cash came from and where it went. ｜ 现金流量表说明现金从哪里来、流向哪里。
  • Operating cash flow is usually the most important signal of underlying business health. ｜ 经营现金流通常最能反映企业基本面。
  • Ratio analysis turns raw financial statements into interpretable performance indicators. ｜ 比率分析把原始报表转化为可解释的经营指标。
  • Comparisons matter: ratios are useful across time or across firms. ｜ 比率的意义来自比较：纵向比较或横向比较都重要。

Important Distinction

  • A profitable firm can still run into cash-flow trouble. ｜ 盈利企业也可能出现现金流危机。
  • Operating cash flow is not the same as net income. ｜ 经营现金流不等于净利润。
  • A high ratio is not always “good” without context. ｜ 没有背景时，高比率不一定就代表好。
  • Ratios simplify reality, so they must be interpreted carefully. ｜ 比率是对现实的简化，因此必须谨慎解读。

Exam Thinking

A strong answer explains why profit and cash can diverge, and then links ratio results back to business performance rather than treating them as isolated formulas.

高分答案要解释利润和现金为什么会背离，并把比率结果重新联系到真实经营，而不是只把它们当成孤立公式。

Week 4

Costing & Breakeven Analysis

成本分类与盈亏平衡分析

Key Concepts

  • Fixed cost ｜ 固定成本
  • Variable cost ｜ 变动成本
  • Semi-variable cost ｜ 混合成本
  • Cost behaviour ｜ 成本性态
  • Contribution margin ｜ 贡献边际
  • Breakeven point ｜ 盈亏平衡点
  • Margin of safety ｜ 安全边际

Core Logic

  • Understanding cost structure helps managers forecast profit and plan output. ｜ 理解成本结构有助于管理者预测利润和规划产出。
  • Contribution margin = selling price – variable cost per unit. ｜ 贡献边际 = 单价 – 单位变动成本。
  • Breakeven occurs when total revenue equals total cost. ｜ 盈亏平衡点出现在总收入等于总成本时。
  • Changes in price, cost, or volume can materially shift profitability. ｜ 价格、成本和销量变化都会显著影响利润。

Important Distinction

  • Cost is context-dependent; not all costs matter for every decision. ｜ 成本具有情境依赖性，不是所有成本都对每个决策相关。
  • Contribution margin is not the same as profit. ｜ 贡献边际不等于利润。
  • A lower price may increase volume, but still reduce overall profit. ｜ 降价可能提高销量，但仍可能降低总利润。
  • Healthcare services also have cost structures, not just factories. ｜ 医疗服务同样有成本结构，不只是制造业才有。

Exam Thinking

In breakeven questions, always connect the calculation to decision-making: what happens if price falls, demand rises, or fixed costs increase?

做盈亏平衡题时，不要只停在计算。一定要继续分析：降价、需求上升或固定成本增加后，决策会发生什么变化。

Week 5

Budgeting & Variance Analysis

预算与差异分析

Key Concepts

  • Budget ｜ 预算
  • Forecast ｜ 预测
  • Master budget ｜ 总预算
  • Flexible budget ｜ 弹性预算
  • Top-down / bottom-up budgeting ｜ 自上而下 / 自下而上预算
  • Favourable / unfavourable variance ｜ 有利 / 不利差异
  • Sales, labour, material variances ｜ 销售、人工、材料差异

Core Logic

  • A budget is a financial plan for future activity. ｜ 预算是对未来活动的财务规划。
  • A flexible budget adjusts expected figures to actual activity levels. ｜ 弹性预算会根据实际业务量调整预算值。
  • Variance analysis compares actual performance with budgeted expectations. ｜ 差异分析比较实际结果与预算目标。
  • The purpose is not just to identify gaps, but to understand why they occurred. ｜ 差异分析的目的不只是找出偏差，更是解释偏差成因。

Important Distinction

  • Budget and forecast are related but not the same. ｜ 预算和预测相关，但不是同一个概念。
  • A favourable variance is not always good in a deeper strategic sense. ｜ 有利差异不一定在更深层战略上就是好事。
  • Static and flexible budgets answer different questions. ｜ 静态预算和弹性预算回答的是不同问题。
  • Variance formulas matter, but interpretation matters more. ｜ 差异公式重要，但解释更重要。

Exam Thinking

A high-quality answer shows both the calculation and the managerial meaning: was the variance caused by volume, price, efficiency, or control failure?

高分答案既要算对，也要说明管理含义：差异到底是销量、价格、效率还是控制失误造成的？

Week 6

Time Value of Money

货币时间价值

Key Concepts

  • Timeline ｜ 时间线
  • Cash inflow / outflow ｜ 现金流入 / 流出
  • Present value (PV) ｜ 现值
  • Future value (FV) ｜ 终值
  • Discounting ｜ 贴现
  • Compounding ｜ 复利
  • Perpetuity ｜ 永续年金
  • Annuity ｜ 年金

Core Logic

  • Cash flows at different points in time cannot be directly compared. ｜ 不同时间点的现金流不能直接比较。
  • To move a cash flow forward, compound it; to move it backward, discount it. ｜ 把现金流往未来移动要复利，往现在移动要贴现。
  • PV and FV are different ways of expressing equivalent value across time. ｜ 现值和终值是对跨期等值关系的两种表达。
  • Timelines help organise and interpret financial problems before calculating. ｜ 时间线能帮助你在计算前先把财务问题组织清楚。

Important Distinction

  • A pound today is not the same as a pound in the future. ｜ 今天的一英镑不等于未来的一英镑。
  • Perpetuity lasts forever; annuity lasts for a fixed number of periods. ｜ 永续年金无限期，普通年金只持续固定期数。
  • Correct timing matters: end of year 1 is also beginning of year 2. ｜ 时间点判断很重要：第1年末也就是第2年初。
  • A timeline error often causes the whole answer to go wrong. ｜ 时间线画错，整个答案通常都会错。

Exam Thinking

Before using formulas, draw the timeline. Many mistakes come from misunderstanding when each cash flow occurs, not from arithmetic.

用公式前先画时间线。很多错误不是算错，而是没搞清楚现金流发生在什么时候。

Week 7

Investment Appraisal: NPV, IRR, Payback & PI

投资决策：NPV、IRR、回收期与PI

Key Concepts

  • NPV rule ｜ 净现值法
  • IRR rule ｜ 内部收益率法
  • Payback rule ｜ 回收期法
  • Mutually exclusive projects ｜ 互斥项目
  • Scale, timing, risk ｜ 规模、时间、风险
  • Profitability index (PI) ｜ 盈利指数
  • Capital constraints ｜ 资本约束

Core Logic

  • For stand-alone projects, accept if NPV > 0. ｜ 对独立项目，若 NPV > 0 则接受。
  • IRR compares a project’s return with its cost of capital. ｜ IRR 把项目回报率与资本成本比较。
  • For mutually exclusive projects, the correct rule is to choose the highest NPV. ｜ 对互斥项目，正确规则是选 NPV 最高者。
  • PI helps rank projects when resources are limited, but it is not perfect. ｜ 当资源有限时，PI有助于排序，但它并不完美。

Important Distinction

  • IRR can mislead when projects differ in scale, timing, or risk. ｜ 当项目在规模、时间或风险上不同时，IRR可能误导。
  • Payback is simple, but ignores time value of money and later cash flows. ｜ 回收期法简单，但忽略时间价值和后期现金流。
  • PI is useful under a single constraint, but can fail with unused capacity or multiple constraints. ｜ PI在单一约束下有用，但在资源闲置或多重约束时会失效。
  • The highest percentage return is not always the highest value creation. ｜ 回报率最高不一定意味着创造价值最多。

Exam Thinking

In investment appraisal, never stop at one rule. Show why NPV is primary, where IRR becomes misleading, and when PI is appropriate.

做投资决策题时，不要只会套一个规则。要说明为什么 NPV 是主规则，IRR 在哪里会误导，以及 PI 适用于什么场景。

Week 8

Sensitivity, Scenario Analysis & Capital Structure

敏感性分析、情景分析与资本结构

Key Concepts

  • Sensitivity analysis ｜ 敏感性分析
  • Scenario analysis ｜ 情景分析
  • Stand-alone risk ｜ 单变量风险分析
  • Combined effects ｜ 多变量联动
  • Capital structure ｜ 资本结构
  • Debt vs equity ｜ 债务与股权
  • MM propositions ｜ MM命题
  • Trade-off theory ｜ 权衡理论

Core Logic

  • Sensitivity analysis changes one variable at a time to see its effect on NPV. ｜ 敏感性分析一次只变一个变量，观察其对NPV的影响。
  • Scenario analysis changes multiple variables together to model coherent states of the world. ｜ 情景分析同时改变多个变量，以模拟更完整的现实情境。
  • Risk analysis improves decision-making by showing uncertainty, not by eliminating it. ｜ 风险分析通过展示不确定性来改进决策，而不是消除不确定性。
  • Capital structure asks how firms mix debt and equity to finance activities. ｜ 资本结构讨论企业如何混合使用债务和股权来融资。

Important Distinction

  • Sensitivity = one variable; scenario = several variables together. ｜ 敏感性分析是单变量，情景分析是多变量联动。
  • Neither method gives a full probability distribution by itself. ｜ 两者本身都不能完整给出概率分布。
  • Debt may be cheaper, but usually brings more financial risk. ｜ 债务可能更便宜，但通常带来更高财务风险。
  • Capital structure theories are frameworks, not automatic answers. ｜ 资本结构理论是分析框架，不是自动答案。

Exam Thinking

Good answers compare tools critically: what each method reveals, what it misses, and why managers need more than one way to think about uncertainty.

好答案要批判性比较不同工具：每种方法能揭示什么、遗漏什么，以及为什么管理者不能只依赖一种不确定性分析方法。

Week 9

Financing Decisions: Equity, Debt & WACC

融资决策：股权、债务与加权资本成本

Key Concepts

  • Private placing ｜ 定向配售
  • IPO ｜ 首次公开募股
  • Rights issue ｜ 配股
  • Bank borrowing ｜ 银行借款
  • Bonds ｜ 债券
  • Venture capital / angel investors ｜ 风投 / 天使投资
  • Crowdfunding ｜ 众筹
  • WACC ｜ 加权平均资本成本

Core Logic

  • Firms can raise capital from both equity and debt sources. ｜ 企业可以通过股权和债务两种方式融资。
  • Different financing sources have different costs, risks, and control implications. ｜ 不同融资方式对应不同成本、风险和控制权影响。
  • WACC combines the cost of debt and equity into one overall required return. ｜ WACC把债务成本和股权成本综合为一个整体资本成本。
  • Financing choices affect not only cost, but also ownership, flexibility, and risk exposure. ｜ 融资决策影响的不只是成本，还包括控制权、灵活性和风险暴露。

Important Distinction

  • Equity does not require fixed interest payments, but dilutes ownership. ｜ 股权融资不需要固定利息，但会稀释所有权。
  • Debt may be cheaper, but creates repayment obligations. ｜ 债务可能更便宜，但会带来偿付义务。
  • IPO, rights issue, and private placing are not the same route to equity finance. ｜ IPO、配股和定向配售不是同一种股权融资路径。
  • WACC is not just a formula; it is a decision benchmark. ｜ WACC不只是公式，它也是投资决策基准。

Exam Thinking

Strong answers compare financing methods in terms of cost, control, speed, flexibility and suitability for the organisation’s context, especially in healthcare.

高分答案会从成本、控制权、融资速度、灵活性以及组织背景适配性等方面比较不同融资方式，尤其要结合医疗情境。

Week 10

Consolidation, Revision & Presentation Thinking

总复习、整合与展示逻辑

What This Week Does

  • Brings together the major ideas from Weeks 1–9. ｜ 把第1到第9周的核心内容整合起来。
  • Emphasises revision, synthesis, and practical application. ｜ 强调整体复习、综合理解和实际应用。
  • Supports preparation for presentations and final exam logic. ｜ 为展示和期末考试思路做准备。

High-Frequency Areas

  • Financial statements and their links ｜ 财务报表及其联动
  • Cash vs profit ｜ 现金与利润
  • Contribution and breakeven ｜ 贡献边际与盈亏平衡
  • Budget vs actual and variance interpretation ｜ 预算与实际差异解释
  • PV / FV / annuity / perpetuity ｜ 现值、终值、年金、永续年金
  • NPV / IRR / Payback / PI ｜ NPV、IRR、回收期、PI
  • Debt, equity, and WACC ｜ 债务、股权与WACC

Revision Strategy

  • Revise by theme, not only by week. ｜ 不要只按周复习，也要按主题复习。
  • Pair formulas with interpretation, not memorisation only. ｜ 公式要和解释一起复习，不能只死记。
  • Use case-based questions to connect accounting and finance logic. ｜ 用案例题把会计与财务逻辑连起来。
  • Practise identifying which decision rule is appropriate in context. ｜ 练习判断在不同情境下该用哪种决策规则。

Exam Thinking

The strongest exam answers do not treat each topic separately. They connect statements, cash, cost, budgeting, investment, and financing into one coherent managerial logic.

最强的考试答案不会把每个主题割裂开来，而是把报表、现金、成本、预算、投资和融资整合成一个连贯的管理逻辑。
