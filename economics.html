Week 1

Climate Change, Data & Economic Thinking

气候变化、数据分析与经济学思维入门

Key Concepts

  • Temperature anomaly ｜ 温度异常值
  • Trend over time ｜ 时间趋势
  • Distribution shift ｜ 分布整体移动
  • CO₂ and temperature ｜ 二氧化碳与气温
  • Correlation vs causation ｜ 相关性与因果性
  • Spurious correlation ｜ 伪相关
  • GDP as a measure ｜ GDP 作为衡量指标
  • Well-being beyond output ｜ 超越产出的福祉理解

Core Logic

  • Temperature anomaly compares current temperature with a long-run average. ｜ 温度异常值用来比较当前温度与长期平均值。
  • Long-run data suggests warming is not random short-term fluctuation. ｜ 长期数据说明变暖不是短期随机波动。
  • Climate evidence is stronger when we examine trend, variation, and distribution together. ｜ 当我们同时看趋势、波动和分布时，证据更强。
  • Economic interpretation often depends on how we measure change over time. ｜ 经济学解释往往依赖于我们如何衡量时间中的变化。

Important Distinction

  • Correlation means two variables move together. ｜ 相关性表示两个变量一起变化。
  • Causation means one variable produces change in another. ｜ 因果性表示一个变量导致另一个变量变化。
  • Economists must be careful not to confuse the two. ｜ 经济学分析必须避免把两者混淆。
  • GDP is useful, but not enough to fully describe social welfare. ｜ GDP 有用，但不足以完整描述社会福利。

Exam Thinking

Do not just say “temperature rises, so climate change is real.” A stronger answer explains trend, compares time scales, and notes that correlation alone does not prove causation.

不要只写“温度上升，所以气候变化存在”。 更好的答案要解释长期趋势、时间尺度差异，并指出相关性不自动等于因果关系。

Week 2

GDP, Well-being, Economic Models & Causality

GDP、幸福感、经济模式与因果推断

Key Concepts

  • GDP ｜ 国内生产总值
  • Productivity ｜ 生产率
  • Externalities ｜ 外部性
  • Well-being ｜ 幸福感 / 福祉
  • Natural experiment ｜ 自然实验
  • Difference-in-differences ｜ 双重差分
  • Money creation ｜ 货币创造
  • Private vs bank-created money ｜ 私人借贷与银行创造货币

Economic Comparison

  • Aurea prioritises time, community, and life satisfaction. ｜ Aurea 重视时间、社群和生活满意度。
  • Pyrgus prioritises output, profit, and industrial growth. ｜ Pyrgus 重视产出、利润和工业增长。
  • High GDP does not automatically mean a better society. ｜ 高GDP并不自动意味着更好的社会。
  • Different systems reflect different social values. ｜ 不同经济系统反映不同的社会价值观。

Economic Models

  • Some systems are based on reciprocity and redistribution. ｜ 有些经济建立在互惠和再分配上。
  • Some systems are designed to avoid state control. ｜ 有些经济安排是为了逃避国家控制。
  • England is best represented by agrarian capitalism and market dependence. ｜ 英格兰最符合农业资本主义和市场依赖模式。
  • Land, property, and surplus are organised differently across models. ｜ 土地、产权和剩余分配在不同模式中组织方式不同。

Cash / Money Logic

  • A private loan transfers existing money. ｜ 私人借贷转移的是已存在的货币。
  • A bank loan can create new money electronically. ｜ 银行贷款可以通过电子记账创造新货币。
  • This helps explain why money in the economy can expand. ｜ 这解释了为什么经济中的货币总量会扩张。
  • Interest raises repayment obligations beyond the original principal. ｜ 利息会使偿还金额高于原始本金。

Exam Thinking

Week 2 is not only about “which economy is richer.” It is about values, measurement, institutions, and whether output should be the only metric of success.

第2周不只是“哪个经济体更有钱”，而是关于价值判断、衡量标准、制度安排，以及产出是否应是唯一成功标准。

Week 3

Supply, Demand, Equilibrium & Identification

供给、需求、均衡与识别问题

Key Concepts

  • Demand curve ｜ 需求曲线
  • Supply curve ｜ 供给曲线
  • Equilibrium price ｜ 均衡价格
  • Exogenous shock ｜ 外生冲击
  • Endogenous movement ｜ 内生变动
  • Simultaneity ｜ 同时性问题
  • Elasticity ｜ 弹性
  • Natural log transformation ｜ 对数变换

Core Logic

  • Demand slopes downward because quantity demanded falls when price rises. ｜ 需求曲线向下，因为价格越高需求量越低。
  • Supply slopes upward because firms are willing to produce more at higher prices. ｜ 供给曲线向上，因为高价格下企业更愿意生产。
  • Equilibrium occurs where supply equals demand. ｜ 均衡出现在供需相等的地方。
  • Shifts in curves matter more than simple movement along a curve. ｜ 曲线移动比沿曲线移动更关键。

Why Real Data Is Hard

  • Observed price and quantity are jointly determined. ｜ 实际观察到的价格和数量是同时决定的。
  • This makes it hard to identify the true supply or demand curve. ｜ 这使得真实供给或需求曲线难以识别。
  • Economists use exogenous shocks to separate them. ｜ 经济学家用外生冲击来帮助识别。
  • Watermelon data is used to show how identification works. ｜ 西瓜市场数据被用来展示识别问题。

Logs

  • Natural log is useful for transforming data. ｜ 自然对数可用于数据转换。
  • In a log-log model, coefficients can be interpreted as elasticities. ｜ 在双对数模型中，系数可解释为弹性。
  • Graphs help economists interpret equations visually. ｜ 图像帮助经济学家从视觉上理解方程。

Exam Thinking

Always distinguish between a movement along a curve and a shift of a curve. This is one of the most common mistakes in early microeconomics.

一定要区分“沿曲线移动”和“整条曲线移动”。 这是微观经济学最常见的错误之一。

Week 4

Inflation, Unemployment & Well-being

通胀、失业与幸福感

Key Concepts

  • CPI ｜ 消费者价格指数
  • GDP deflator ｜ GDP平减指数
  • Inflation / deflation / disinflation ｜ 通胀 / 通缩 / 通胀回落
  • Aggregate demand ｜ 总需求
  • Phillips curve ｜ 菲利普斯曲线
  • Life satisfaction ｜ 生活满意度
  • Confidence interval ｜ 置信区间
  • Correlation coefficient ｜ 相关系数

Core Logic

  • Inflation is a general rise in prices. ｜ 通胀是总体价格水平上升。
  • Unemployment matters not only for income, but also for dignity and well-being. ｜ 失业不仅影响收入，也影响尊严和幸福感。
  • Life satisfaction is often used as a proxy for non-monetary costs. ｜ 生活满意度常被用作非货币成本的代理指标。
  • Economic data can help estimate social costs we cannot directly observe. ｜ 经济数据可以帮助估计那些无法直接观察的社会成本。

Policy

  • Fiscal policy uses taxes, spending, and transfers. ｜ 财政政策通过税收、支出和转移支付发挥作用。
  • Monetary policy mainly uses the interest rate. ｜ 货币政策主要通过利率发挥作用。
  • Negative demand shocks often require expansionary policy. ｜ 负向需求冲击往往需要扩张性政策。
  • Social norms can affect how unemployment changes life satisfaction. ｜ 社会规范会影响失业如何改变生活满意度。

Exam Thinking

CPI and GDP deflator are not the same: CPI focuses on consumer purchases, while GDP deflator focuses on domestic production.

CPI 和 GDP 平减指数不一样：CPI 关注消费者购买，GDP 平减指数关注国内生产。

Week 5

Firms, Labour, Wages & Employment Rent

企业、劳动、工资与就业租

Key Concepts

  • Profit maximisation ｜ 利润最大化
  • Fixed / variable / marginal cost ｜ 固定 / 可变 / 边际成本
  • Reservation wage ｜ 保留工资
  • Employment rent ｜ 就业租
  • Principal-agent problem ｜ 委托代理问题
  • Labour productivity ｜ 劳动生产率
  • Real wage ｜ 实际工资
  • Economies of scale ｜ 规模经济

Firm Decisions

  • Marketing focuses on price and quantity. ｜ 市场部门关注价格和数量。
  • HR focuses on wage-setting and labour discipline. ｜ 人力部门关注工资与劳动管理。
  • Profit-maximising output is chosen where the relevant conditions are satisfied. ｜ 企业在满足利润最大化条件时选择最优产量。
  • Costs shape how firms behave in markets. ｜ 成本结构决定企业在市场中的行为。

Labour Market

  • Reservation wage is the minimum wage a worker accepts. ｜ 保留工资是劳动者愿意接受的最低工资。
  • Employment rent is the extra benefit of keeping the job. ｜ 就业租是保住工作的额外收益。
  • Better benefits and worse outside options increase employment rent. ｜ 福利更好、外部选择更差时，就业租更高。
  • Monitoring and incentives matter because effort is not always observable. ｜ 监督和激励很重要，因为劳动努力并不总是可观察的。

Exam Thinking

A high wage does not automatically mean a high employment rent. You must compare the value of the job with the value of the next-best option.

高工资不一定意味着高就业租。 关键是比较这份工作与次优选择之间的差异。

Week 6

Inequality, Lorenz Curves & Gini

不平等、洛伦兹曲线与基尼系数

Key Concepts

  • Income inequality ｜ 收入不平等
  • Wealth inequality ｜ 财富不平等
  • Lorenz curve ｜ 洛伦兹曲线
  • Gini coefficient ｜ 基尼系数
  • Redistribution ｜ 再分配
  • Predistribution ｜ 事前分配
  • Categorical inequality ｜ 类别型不平等
  • Intergenerational inequality ｜ 代际不平等

Core Logic

  • Wealth is usually more unequal than income. ｜ 财富通常比收入分布更不均。
  • Disposable income is often more equal because of taxes and transfers. ｜ 由于税收和转移支付，可支配收入通常更平均。
  • The farther the Lorenz curve is from equality, the greater inequality is. ｜ 洛伦兹曲线离完全平等线越远，不平等越严重。
  • Inequality can be measured, compared, and debated. ｜ 不平等既可以测量，也可以比较和争论。

Types of Inequality

  • Categorical inequality ｜ 类别型不平等
  • Inherited / intergenerational inequality ｜ 代际不平等
  • Within-country and between-country inequality ｜ 国内与国别间不平等
  • Wealth and income do not move exactly the same way over time. ｜ 财富和收入不平等随时间的变化并不完全一致。

Exam Thinking

Not all inequality is judged equally unfair. A strong answer explains not just how much inequality exists, but where it comes from and how policy can address it.

并非所有不平等都被视为同样不公平。 好答案不仅要说明不平等有多严重，还要解释其来源及政策应对方式。

Week 7

Game Theory, Nash Equilibrium & Prisoners’ Dilemma

博弈论、纳什均衡与囚徒困境

Key Concepts

  • Players ｜ 参与者
  • Strategies ｜ 策略
  • Payoffs ｜ 收益
  • Best response ｜ 最佳回应
  • Nash equilibrium ｜ 纳什均衡
  • Dominant strategy ｜ 占优策略
  • Mixed strategy ｜ 混合策略
  • Pareto efficiency ｜ 帕累托效率

Core Logic

  • A Nash equilibrium is self-enforcing. ｜ 纳什均衡是自我维持的。
  • A dominant strategy is best regardless of what the other player does. ｜ 占优策略是不管对方怎么做都最优的策略。
  • Strategic interaction means outcomes depend on others’ actions too. ｜ 战略互动意味着结果取决于他人的行动。
  • In many games, individually rational choices can lead to poor collective outcomes. ｜ 在许多博弈中，个体理性选择会导致糟糕的集体结果。

Prisoners’ Dilemma

  • Individual incentives can conflict with the common good. ｜ 个人激励可能与集体利益冲突。
  • The Nash equilibrium can be inefficient. ｜ 纳什均衡可能是低效率的。
  • Pareto-efficient outcomes are not always chosen. ｜ 帕累托有效结果不一定会被选择。
  • Game type depends on payoff structure. ｜ 博弈类型取决于收益结构。

Exam Thinking

Do not memorise only the definition. You need to read a payoff matrix, identify best responses, and explain why an outcome is or is not efficient.

不要只背定义。 你需要会读收益矩阵、找最佳回应，并解释为什么某个结果有效率或无效率。

Week 8

Market Structure, Monopoly & Market Failure

市场结构、垄断与市场失灵

Key Concepts

  • Perfect competition ｜ 完全竞争
  • Monopoly ｜ 垄断
  • Oligopoly ｜ 寡头
  • Monopolistic competition ｜ 垄断竞争
  • Deadweight loss ｜ 无谓损失
  • Market failure ｜ 市场失灵
  • Free riding ｜ 搭便车
  • Asymmetric information ｜ 信息不对称

Core Logic

  • Perfect competition is the efficiency benchmark. ｜ 完全竞争是效率基准。
  • A monopolist restricts output and raises price. ｜ 垄断者会限制产量并提高价格。
  • Monopoly creates deadweight loss because some mutually beneficial trades do not happen. ｜ 垄断会带来无谓损失，因为一些本可实现的交易消失了。
  • Market structure affects incentives, welfare, and pricing power. ｜ 市场结构会影响激励、福利和定价权。

Further Structures

  • Oligopoly involves strategic interaction between few firms. ｜ 寡头市场中少数企业相互博弈。
  • Monopolistic competition combines many firms with product differentiation. ｜ 垄断竞争是多企业加产品差异化。
  • Healthcare markets often contain market power and imperfect competition. ｜ 医疗市场常常存在市场力量和不完全竞争。
  • Bertrand and Cournot are common oligopoly models. ｜ Bertrand 和 Cournot 是常见寡头模型。

Exam Thinking

When comparing perfect competition and monopoly, always talk about price, output, surplus, and deadweight loss together.

比较完全竞争和垄断时，一定要把价格、产量、剩余和无谓损失放在一起说。

Week 9

Commons, Governance & Economic Evaluation

公地、治理与经济评价

Key Concepts

  • Private / public / club / common goods ｜ 私人物品 / 公共物品 / 俱乐部物品 / 公地物品
  • Tragedy of the commons ｜ 公地悲剧
  • Free riding ｜ 搭便车
  • Ostrom self-governance ｜ Ostrom自组织治理
  • WTP ｜ 支付意愿
  • Cost-benefit analysis ｜ 成本收益分析
  • Externalities ｜ 外部性
  • Tax and markets ｜ 税收与市场起源

Core Logic

  • Common goods are rival but non-excludable. ｜ 公地物品具有竞争性但不可排他。
  • Mainstream solutions often contrast market and state solutions. ｜ 主流经济学常把市场方案和国家方案对立起来。
  • Ostrom shows that communities can also self-organise. ｜ Ostrom 说明社群也可能自我组织治理。
  • Economic organisation is shaped by institutions, not just markets. ｜ 经济组织形式不仅由市场决定，也由制度决定。

Economic Evaluation

  • Governments often compare alternatives through costs and benefits. ｜ 政府常通过成本与收益比较不同方案。
  • WTP is used to estimate benefits. ｜ 支付意愿用于估计收益。
  • CBA tries to guide public decisions using economic valuation. ｜ 成本收益分析试图用经济价值引导公共决策。
  • Some goods with positive externalities may justify state involvement. ｜ 一些具有正外部性的物品可能需要国家介入。

Exam Thinking

Week 9 is not only about “commons are overused.” It is also about who governs resources, how rules emerge, and whether markets are natural or historically constructed.

第9周不只是“公地会被过度使用”。 它还讨论谁来治理资源、规则如何形成，以及市场究竟是不是自然形成的。

Week 10

Revision & Mock Exam Logic

总复习与模拟题逻辑

What This Week Does

  • Reviews the previous 9 lectures. ｜ 回顾前9周内容。
  • Uses mixed practice questions to test understanding. ｜ 通过混合练习题检测理解。
  • Connects concepts across weeks. ｜ 把不同周的知识串联起来。

High-Frequency Topics

  • Competitive equilibrium ｜ 竞争均衡
  • CPI and inflation ｜ CPI与通胀
  • Employment rent ｜ 就业租
  • Commons and Ostrom ｜ 公地与Ostrom
  • Nash equilibrium ｜ 纳什均衡
  • Monopoly and deadweight loss ｜ 垄断与无谓损失
  • Money creation ｜ 货币创造
  • Supply and demand shifts ｜ 供需曲线移动

Revision Strategy

  • Do not revise week by week only; revise by theme. ｜ 不要只按周复习，也要按主题复习。
  • Practice diagrams, definitions, and applied MCQs together. ｜ 要把图、定义和应用选择题一起练。
  • Look for repeated logic: incentives, efficiency, institutions, and welfare. ｜ 重点寻找重复出现的逻辑：激励、效率、制度与福利。
  • Mock questions reveal which ideas are repeatedly tested. ｜ 模拟题能显示哪些概念是高频考点。

Exam Thinking

The final exam rewards connections. A strong answer often links market structure, incentives, efficiency, and fairness instead of treating each idea in isolation.

期末考试奖励“连接能力”。 强答案通常会把市场结构、激励、效率和公平联系起来，而不是各讲各的。
